Little Lifely — Q2 Planning

Where we are. Where we're going. What it takes.

📖 The Story So Far

Six months ago, Little Lifely didn't exist as a standalone business. It was a product on lifely.com.au.

In October, we did $132K. By March, we hit $1.33M in a single month. That's 10x in 6 months.

Q1 total: $3.12M — beating our target by 6%. USA exploded to 33% of revenue. We proved we can sell an Australian kids' bed to American parents at $639 USD with a 44% contribution margin.

Now the question: can we double it again in Q2? The financial model says $6.4M. That's aggressive. But the trajectory says it's possible — if we fix what's breaking.

🏆 Q1 Wins — What Went Right

🚀 +117% revenue QoQ — $1.44M (Q4) → $3.12M (Q1)
🇺🇸 USA grew 239% — from $305K to $1.04M. One-third of all LL revenue now.
📈 Beat Q1 target by 6% — AU +7%, US +11%, CA +5%
🇨🇦 Canada = most efficient market — 6.8x ROAS, $106 CPA (best of any region)
💰 CVR improving — 0.47% (Jan) → 0.63% (Mar), up 34%
📧 Emily's welcome email flow — $71K in 30 days. Email ATC rate is 11.4% (7x better than ads)
📉 Refund rate dropping fast — 10.1% (Jan) → 2.1% (Mar)
📦 Inventory healthy — 91 days of stock cover, 4x annual turnover

⚠️ Q1 Concerns — What Needs Fixing

🐌 Mobile page speed: 28/100 — LCP is 34.8 seconds. 93% of traffic is mobile. This is the #1 conversion killer.
💸 CPA rose 54% — $115 (Jan) → $177 (Mar). We're paying more for each customer.
📉 Ad fatigue setting in — CTR dropped from 2.53% → 2.04%. CPC rose from $0.65 → $0.73.
🔗 Landing page mismatch — Meta traffic to landing page: 0.8% ATC. Direct to PDP: 5.4%. That's a 6.75x gap.
🇺🇸 US ATC rate is lowest — 2.6% vs AU 3.1%, CA 3.8%, SG 4.9%
📉 AOV declining — $897 (Jan) → $808 (Mar). Fewer bundles? More single items?
💀 March engagement collapsed — Bounce rate spiked to 74% (was 61%). Engagement dropped to 26%.
🔄 Repeat rate only 2% — 98% of customers buy once and leave

❓ The Q2 Question

The model says $5.84M. That's AU $3.05M + US $2.21M + CA $394K + ROW $180K.

To get there, we need to roughly double Q1. Can we do it?

The bull case: Trajectory is there (10x in 6 months). US is still early. Canada is underinvested. Email channel barely tapped. Product quiz ready to deploy.

The bear case: CPA is rising fast. Page speed is broken. Creative is fatiguing. June target ($3.06M in one month) requires 2.3x March performance. Seasonality says April dips.

Today's discussion: What are the 3 objectives that give us the best shot?
