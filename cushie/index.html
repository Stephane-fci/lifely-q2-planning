Cushie — Q2 Planning

Q1 scorecard. What's working. What needs attention. Where Q2 goes.

📖 The Story So Far

Cushie is the only Category Killer with its own standalone site — cushiehome.com. It sells modular sleeper sofas in the US market, competing against IKEA, Article, and traditional sleeper sofas.

Q1 total: $592K (USD) across 640 orders. Revenue grew every month — +14% Jan→Feb, +21% Feb→Mar. That's a healthy upward trajectory.

Meta Ads drove $102K in spend at a blended 3.3x ROAS and 363 attributed purchases. The Branded Post campaign is the star at 7.2x ROAS.

But there are signals worth watching: discounts are heavy ($177K in Q1 — 30% of gross), CPA spiked in March ($321), and the Snuggle line is scaling fast but with lower efficiency.

🏆 Q1 Wins — What Went Right

📈 +36% revenue growth Jan→Mar — $171K → $231K. Consistent month-over-month gains.
🛒 640 total orders — 182 → 211 → 247. Order volume accelerating every month.
💰 $924 blended AOV — strong for a US DTC furniture brand.
🎯 Branded Post campaign at 7.2x ROAS — $9.5K spend driving $68K in purchases. Most efficient campaign.
🆕 Snuggle line gaining traction — 121 units, $176K revenue. New product line proving demand.
👩 Female 25-34 = best ROAS — 4.2x on $11.5K spend. Most efficient demo segment.
🇺🇸 99% US revenue — laser-focused market. No geographic dilution.

⚠️ Q1 Concerns — What Needs Attention

💸 CPA spiked to $321 in March — was $289 in Jan. 11% increase while spend went up 41%.
📉 March ROAS dropped to 2.9x — from 3.3x in Jan and 3.9x in Feb. Efficiency declining as spend scales.
🏷️ Heavy discounting: $177K in Q1 — 30% of gross revenue given as discounts. Sustainable?
🔄 48 refund events in Q1 — $28K refunded (4.7% of revenue). Off-gassing is #1 review complaint.
🧪 Creative testing at 2.4x ROAS — $19K spend, underperforming. Are we testing the right creatives?
🇨🇦 Canada barely started — $2.3K spend, 3 purchases. Is there a bigger opportunity here?
📊 No GA4 property connected — can't measure funnel metrics (sessions, ATC rate, CVR). Flying partially blind.

❓ The Q2 Question

Cushie did $592K in Q1 with $102K in Meta spend.

The trajectory is positive (+36% Jan→Mar). But ROAS is declining as spend scales — the classic scaling tension.

The bull case: March hit $231K organically. Branded Post campaign is insanely efficient (7.2x). Snuggle line is a new growth vector. Canada is untapped. The product has 4.72★ on 117 reviews.

The bear case: CPA jumped 11% in one month. Heavy discounting (30%) pressures margins. No GA4 = no funnel visibility. Creative testing isn't producing clear winners yet. Off-gassing reviews could become a brand risk.

Today's discussion: Can we grow to $800K+ in Q2 while improving unit economics?

💡 Key Insight
Facebook gets 62% of spend but Instagram's per-impression efficiency is higher (3.2x ROAS on fewer impressions).
March CTR jumped to 7.9% (from 3.5% Jan) while CPC dropped to $0.26 — creative resonance is improving, but conversions aren't keeping pace. This suggests a landing page or offer problem, not an ad problem.

⚡ Meta Ads Strategic Signals

📈 CTR tripled (3.5% → 7.9%) while ROAS dropped (3.3x → 2.9x). Ads are getting clicks but not converting. Diagnose the post-click experience.
💰 Branded Post at 7.2x is underleveraged — only $9.5K of $102K total spend. Scale this first.
🔬 Creative Testing spending $19K at 2.4x — test more aggressively but kill losers faster. ABO should graduate winners to ASC.
🆕 Snuggle ASC Creative Test at 4.0x — best new campaign. Proof that Snuggle resonates with cold traffic.
👩 Female 25-34 is the efficiency sweet spot — 4.2x ROAS on lowest CPA. Under-indexed on spend ($11.5K vs $18K for 35-44).
🇨🇦 Canada needs patience or pause — $2.3K spent, 3 purchases. Too early to judge but economics don't work yet.

💬 Q2 Planning Workshop

✅ Keep Doing

  • Branded Post campaign approach — 7.2x ROAS, by far the most efficient
  • Snuggle line expansion — proving product-market fit at $1,455 AOV
  • US market focus — 98% of revenue, deep understanding of the buyer
  • Month-over-month order growth — 182→211→247 is a healthy curve

🛑 Stop / Fix

  • Don't scale spend faster than creative output — March CPA spiked because spend +41% but new winners didn't keep up
  • Don't let discounting creep past 23% — already at 30% of gross. Need a margin-aware promo calendar
  • Don't ignore off-gassing complaints — it's #1 in Junip reviews. Proactive brand response needed
  • Canada ads at 0.9x ROAS — pause until US is optimized or test completely different approach

🧪 Try in Q2

  • Google Ads PMax — $0 Google spend in Q1. Huge untapped channel for high-intent shoppers.
  • Sectional upsell path — 19 orders at $2,891 avg. Can 2-seater buyers become sectional buyers?
  • Email/SMS flows — Klaviyo connected but no data on Cushie email performance yet. Set up welcome + abandon flows.
  • UGC creative pipeline — Current creatives are aging. Fresh UGC for Snuggle + 2-Seater.
  • Advertorials for Cushie — worked for other CKs. Sleeper sofa angle + apartment living.
