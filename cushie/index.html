📖 The Q1 Story

Cushie did $1.04M USD equivalent in Q1 across 4 countries and 2 products:

• 🇺🇸 US (cushiehome.com): $586K USD - beat forecast by $10K overall, Cushie V2 missed target (-12%), Snuggle smashed it (+124%)
• 🇦🇺 AU (lifely.com.au): $434K AUD net - Cushie V2 MISSED target by -36%, Snuggle beat target (+6%)
• 🇨🇦 CA (lifely.com.au): $28K AUD - Cushie barely started, Snuggle breaking out (2→5→18 orders)
• 🇳🇿 NZ (lifely.com.au): $8K AUD - declining, not a priority

The model target for Q1 Cushie V2 AU alone was $430K AUD. We did $274K net. That's a -36% miss. Snuggle AU did $160K AUD net - slightly beat its $151K target.

🏆 Q1 Wins

  • Snuggle US: +124% vs target ($195K tgt → $436K actual AUD)
  • US Cushie MER accelerating - 2.4x → 3.7x → 4.2x (Meta + Google)
  • Canada Snuggle breakout - 2→5→11 Snuggle orders
  • AU Snuggle Google PMax: 14.6x ROAS - most efficient channel
  • 1,077 total orders across 4 countries
  • AU Cushie stable at ~4.5x MER (Meta + Google combined)

⚠️ Q1 Concerns

  • AU Cushie V2: -34% vs target ($430K tgt → $282K actual)
  • US Cushie V2: -11% vs target ($818K tgt → $736K actual AUD)
  • US Snuggle MER collapsed 8.6x → 2.1x (Meta + Google) as spend scaled
  • March US Snuggle CPA: $376 (from $154 Jan)
  • 23% discount rate on US store
  • Google Ads now visible - $139K AUD Q1 across both accounts ✅
