Cushie — Q2 Planning

Two stores. Four countries. One brand. Here's the full picture.

📖 The Full Picture

Cushie sells through two storefronts: cushiehome.com (USD, mainly US) and lifely.com.au (AUD, mainly AU + CA + NZ). Until now we were only looking at the US store.

The real Q1 picture is much bigger:
🇺🇸 US: $592K USD (640 orders) via cushiehome.com
🇦🇺 AU: $389K AUD (379 orders) via lifely.com.au
🇨🇦 CA: $28K AUD (25 orders) via lifely.com.au — exploding in March (2→5→18 orders)
🇳🇿 NZ: $8K AUD (6 orders) via lifely.com.au

Combined: 1,050 orders across 4 countries. And the AU ad account is running Cushie campaigns at 6.5x ROAS — nearly double the US account's 3.3x.

🏆 Q1 Wins

🌏 1,050 total Cushie orders across 4 countries — much bigger than the 640 US-only view.
📈 Every region growing month-over-month — US +36%, AU steady with higher AOV ($1,026 AUD).
🇦🇺 AU Meta Ads at 6.5x ROAS — $84K spend driving $546K in attributed revenue. Snuggle campaigns at 7.4x.
🇨🇦 Canada 9x order growth — 2 orders in Jan → 18 in March. Something is catching on.
💰 $186K combined Meta spend generating 986 attributed purchases. Healthy machine.
🆕 Snuggle line dominating AU — Snuggle campaigns alone did $310K AUD revenue on $42K spend (7.4x).

⚠️ Q1 Concerns

💸 US CPA spiked to $321 in March — 3.3x ROAS overall, declining as spend scales.
📉 AU ROAS dropping too — 7.0x (Jan) → 7.4x (Feb) → 5.1x (Mar). March spend +12% but purchases -23%.
🏷️ US discounting at 23% — $177K in Q1 discounts on cushiehome.com. Margin pressure.
🔄 48 refund events on US store — $28K refunded (4.7%). Off-gassing #1 complaint.
📊 No GA4 for cushiehome.com — can't see funnel metrics for the US store.
🇳🇿 NZ declining — 2→3→1 orders. Small but going wrong direction.

❓ The Q2 Question

Two very different stories in two markets:

Australia is efficient — 6.5x ROAS, strong AOV ($1,026), and Snuggle is a machine. The question is: how much can we scale AU spend before efficiency drops?

US is growing fast but less efficient — 3.3x ROAS, declining in March. The question is: can we fix the post-click experience to convert more of those 5M+ impressions?

Canada just woke up — 2→5→18 orders. The question is: is this organic momentum or the start of a real market?

Today: What's the right spend allocation across regions? Where do we push, where do we optimize?

⚡ Cross-Market Ad Signals

🇦🇺 AU is 2x more efficient than US — 6.5x vs 3.3x ROAS. Same brand, different market dynamics. AU customers convert at higher rates with less discounting.
🆕 Snuggle outperforms Cushie in both markets — AU Snuggle at 7.4x vs Cushie at 5.6x. US Snuggle ASC at 4.0x. Snuggle might be the lead product going forward.
📉 Both markets declining in March — US 3.3→2.9x, AU 7.0→5.1x. Could be seasonal (end of summer AU), creative fatigue, or competition ramping.
🔥 MOFU campaigns are insanely efficient — AU Cushie MOFU at 19.1x, Snuggle MOFU at 25.1x. These are retargeting gold. Max them out.
🇨🇦 CA Snuggle at 6.9x on $3.5K spend — small but promising. Worth testing more budget here in Q2.
💡 US Branded Post at 7.2x is the outlier — only $9.5K of $102K goes here. Scale this before anything else.

💬 Q2 Planning Workshop

✅ Keep Doing

  • AU Snuggle campaigns — 7.4x ROAS, the most efficient thing we run
  • US Branded Post approach — 7.2x ROAS on only $9.5K spend
  • Multi-region expansion — CA growing 9x proves the model travels
  • MOFU retargeting in AU — 19-25x ROAS. Max budget on these

🛑 Stop / Fix

  • US spending $35K/mo on ASC Scale at 2.9x — needs creative refresh or restructure
  • US discounting at 23% — killing margins. Need promotion calendar
  • AU March decline — purchases dropped 23% while spend held. Diagnose why
  • Flying blind on US funnel — no GA4 means no idea where we're losing people

🧪 Try in Q2

  • Google Ads PMax for US — $0 Google spend. High-intent channel untapped.
  • Snuggle as lead product for CA/US cold traffic — it's what's converting in CA
  • Chair Bed as gateway funnel — low price ($722), high volume in US. Entry → upsell
  • Advertorials for Cushie — "apartment living" + "guest room solution" angles
  • Cross-sell from 2-Seater → Sectional — 19 orders at $2,891 AOV. Email flow?
  • Invest more in CA — March showed 18 orders on tiny spend. Test $5K/mo
